Constitution of the Republic of Southcliff

Preamble

We, the citizens of the Republic of Southcliff, in order to establish an enduring community built on freedom, justice, and collaboration, do hereby ordain and establish this Constitution.

Article I: The Nation & Sovereignty

Section 1 (Name): The official name of this sovereign entity is the Republic of Southcliff.

Section 2 (Territory): The territory encompasses all digital properties under the republicofsouthcliff.org domain and any designated lands.

Section 3 (Symbols): National symbols shall be defined by executive decree.

Article II: Citizens & Rights

Section 1: Citizenship is open to all individuals who apply through official channels.

Section 2: Freedom of expression is guaranteed to all citizens.

Section 3: All citizens are equal before the law.

Article III: Executive Power

The Founder holds ultimate executive authority of the Republic of Southcliff.

Article IV: Legislative & Judicial Powers

Legislation may be proposed by citizens and reviewed under constitutional authority.

Article V: Amendments

Amendments may be proposed and must be ratified by the Founder.
