Constitution of the Republic of Southcliff

Preamble

We, the citizens of the Republic of Southcliff, in order to establish an enduring community built on freedom, justice, and collaboration, do hereby ordain and establish this Constitution. Founded on this 10th day of June, 2026, by our Supreme Founder, we pledge to uphold the principles of liberty, equality, and progress for all who claim allegiance to our nation.

Article I: The Nation & Sovereignty

Section 1 (Name): The official name of this sovereign entity is the Republic of Southcliff.

Section 2 (Territory): The territory encompasses all digital properties under the republicofsouthcliff.org domain, alongside any physical lands or claims designated by the state.

Section 3 (Symbols): The official national symbols shall be defined by executive decree of the Founder.

Article II: Citizens & Rights

Section 1 (Citizenship): Citizenship is open to all individuals who apply through official channels.

Section 2 (Freedom of Expression): Every citizen enjoys the right to free speech and peaceful assembly.

Section 3 (Equality): All citizens are equal before the law.

Article III: Executive Power

The Founder holds ultimate executive authority of the state.

Article IV: Legislative & Judicial Powers

Legislation may be proposed by citizens and subject to approval processes defined by the state.

Article V: Amendments

Amendments may be proposed and must be ratified by the Founder.
